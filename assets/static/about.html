This website presents a taxonomy of 76 design flaws in data visualization, categorized into three main categories and ten subcategories (found in the Taxonomy tab). Additionally, the original corpus can be accessed in the Corpus tab.

We further conducted a series of focus group studies to identify the root causes of these design flaws. We also proposed a research agenda to address them, summarizing nine future research opportunities in combating visualization design flaws.

For more detail, please read our paper!
